24. Zinc and vegetarians (observational)

ex0524 <- read.csv('ascii/ex0524.csv')
names(ex0524) <- tolower(names(ex0524))

Doesn't look like much evidence:

ggplot(ex0524, aes(group, zinc)) + geom_boxplot()

The residuals in the "just control" group are calculated in reference to the mean of the whole control group. So the plot shows that the observations jump around together from day to day. So the day seems to have an affect on the protein measurement.

The ten groups do not have equal means [shorthand for "we reject the null hypothesis that the ten groups have equal means"]:

ex0518 <- sleuth.read('ascii/ex0518.csv')
six <- aov(protein ~ treatmnt, ex0518)
ten <- aov(protein ~ trt.daygroup, ex0518)
anova(ten)
## Analysis of Variance Table

## Response: protein
